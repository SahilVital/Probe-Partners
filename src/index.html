Our Panel

What is Research Panel

A research panel refers to a group of individuals who have agreed to participate in surveys, interviews, or other research activities conducted by researchers, companies, or organizations. These individuals, known as panelists, are typically selected based on specific criteria relevant to the research objectives. Research panels can be created for various purposes, including market research, academic studies, product testing, public opinion polling, and more. Panelists may be recruited through various channels, such as online platforms, email invitations, social media, or even in-person recruitment methods.
